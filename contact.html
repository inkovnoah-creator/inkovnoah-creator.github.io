Contact FAQ

Common questions before requesting a freight quote from China.

What information should I send for a quote?

Please send product name, product photos, carton quantity, carton size, gross weight, supplier city and destination address or ZIP code. A packing list is helpful if available.

Should I contact by WhatsApp or email?

WhatsApp is faster for photos and quick questions. Email is better for packing lists, invoices and detailed shipment documents.

Can you pick up from my supplier in China?

Yes. We can help arrange supplier pickup or warehouse receiving in China. Please provide the supplier city or pickup address.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes. We support Amazon FBA delivery, labeling and preparation support when needed.

Can you check sensitive cargo before shipping?

Yes. Please send product photos and details if your cargo includes batteries, cosmetics, liquids, powders, magnets, branded goods or other sensitive items.
