Frequently asked questions

Common questions before arranging international shipping from China.

What does DDP shipping mean?

DDP shipping means customs clearance and import tax handling are included in the shipping solution. Final pricing depends on cargo type, route and destination.

Can you pick up from my supplier in China?

Yes. Pickup or warehouse receiving in China can be arranged. You can also ask your supplier to send goods to our warehouse for consolidation and preparation.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes. We support Amazon FBA delivery, labeling and preparation support when needed. Please send the FBA destination, carton details and product information for checking.

How do I get an accurate quote?

Please provide product name, product photos, carton quantity, carton size, gross weight, supplier city and destination address or ZIP code.

Can you ship batteries, cosmetics or branded goods?

Some sensitive cargo can be handled after review. Please send photos, product description, packaging details and quantity before shipping so we can check the correct route.
