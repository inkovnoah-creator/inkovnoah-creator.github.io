Different goods need different routes. We check product type, weight, address, timing and possible restrictions before recommending a route.

Air Freight DDP

For e-commerce restocking, medium cartons and time-sensitive shipments that need a balance between speed and cost.

  • Faster than sea freight
  • Door delivery when DDP is available
  • Good for sellers and restocking

Sea Freight DDP

For heavier cartons, bulk goods and planned inventory replenishment where cost control matters more than speed.

  • Lower unit cost
  • Suitable for larger volume
  • Planned delivery schedule

Express Shipping

For samples, documents and urgent small parcels where speed is more important than the lowest price.

  • Fast international delivery
  • Tracking support
  • Good for urgent parcels
