Choose the right China to Greece DDP route.

Greece shipments can use different routes depending on cargo type, chargeable weight, carton size, delivery timing and final address. The time ranges below are practical references, not fixed promises.

Faster option

Air DDP to Greece

For parcels and cartons that need a faster door-to-door option.

  • Estimated time: about 12–15 days
  • Suitable for small and medium shipments
  • Customs clearance and tax included when the route and cargo are suitable
Balanced route

Truck DDP to Greece

For planned cargo where cost control is important, but sea freight may be too slow.

  • Estimated time: about 30–35 days
  • Suitable for e-commerce goods, cartons and planned restocking
  • Route availability depends on destination, schedule and product type
Planned cargo

Rail DDP to Greece

For non-urgent general cargo that needs a door-to-door solution with a planned timeline.

  • Estimated time: about 40–45 days
  • Useful for suitable general cargo and larger cartons
  • Customs, transfer and local delivery timing may affect arrival
Lower cost

Sea DDP to Greece

For heavier, bulkier and non-urgent shipments that need cost control.

  • Estimated time: around 60 days
  • Suitable for planned shipments and larger cargo
  • Best for buyers who can plan inventory in advance
