Shipping time & service options

How long does shipping from China to the USA take?

For China-to-USA shipments, express shipping is usually 3–7 days, air freight DDP is usually 8–15 days, and sea freight DDP is usually 20–60 days. The final timeline depends on route, cargo type, customs, destination and carrier transfer.

Which service should I choose: air, sea or express?

Choose express for urgent small parcels and samples, air freight DDP for a balance of speed and cost, and sea freight DDP for bulk cargo or non-urgent inventory shipments.

Do you provide door-to-door delivery?

Yes. We provide door-to-door shipping solutions from China to overseas addresses. For DDP routes, customs clearance, tax handling and final delivery are included in the shipping solution.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes. We support Amazon FBA shipments, including delivery to FBA warehouses and preparation support such as labeling or appointment assistance when needed.

DDP, tax & customs clearance

What does DDP shipping mean?

DDP means Delivered Duty Paid. For supported routes, the shipping solution includes customs clearance and import tax handling, so the cargo can be delivered to the destination address more easily.

Is tax and customs clearance included?

Yes. Our DDP service includes tax handling and customs clearance for supported routes and cargo types. Final availability and pricing depend on the cargo details, destination and route.

Do I need to handle customs myself?

For DDP service, we help arrange customs clearance and tax handling as part of the shipping solution. For special cargo, additional documents or product information may be required.

Can you handle sensitive cargo customs checking?

Some sensitive cargo can be handled after review. Please send product photos, product name, material, packaging, quantity and any special details such as battery, liquid, powder, magnet or brand.

Accepted goods & prohibited goods

What goods can you usually ship?

We can usually help with general goods, e-commerce products, household items, clothing, accessories, toys, samples, personal parcels and many business shipments. Some sensitive cargo such as cosmetics, skincare products, lithium battery products or branded goods must be checked before shipping.

Can you ship cosmetics, batteries or branded goods?

Many sensitive goods can be handled after route and compliance checking. Please send product photos, packaging details, product description, quantity and destination before shipping.

What goods cannot be shipped?

We cannot accept illegal goods, controlled substances, stolen items, weapons, ammunition, military supplies, explosives, dangerous goods, or items that fail customs, safety or route compliance review. Powder, military supplies and drones may be prohibited depending on route and cargo details.

Do you accept personal parcels and gifts?

Yes. We can help with many personal parcels, gifts and personal effects from China. Please provide photos, description, weight, size and destination before shipping.

Quote, pickup & warehouse support

How can I get an accurate shipping quote?

Please provide destination country, city or ZIP code, product name, product photos, carton quantity, carton size, gross weight, supplier city, and preferred shipping method. A packing list is helpful if available.

Can you pick up goods from my supplier in China?

Yes. We provide pickup support across mainland China. Your supplier can also send the goods to our Yiwu warehouse for receiving, checking, packing or consolidation.

Do you provide free services?

Yes. We can provide free quality inspection, packing support and short-term warehousing in Yiwu. Availability may depend on cargo type, quantity and service arrangement.

Can you consolidate goods from multiple suppliers?

Yes. We can receive goods from multiple suppliers in China, check and consolidate them before arranging international shipping.

Tracking, insurance & support

How can I track my shipment?

You can use the tracking page on our website or contact us with your tracking number. Tracking information may take time to update after pickup, customs clearance or transfer to the local carrier.

What happens if cargo is lost or damaged?

For standard compensation, air freight compensation is 40 RMB/kg plus freight refund, and sea freight compensation is 20 RMB/kg plus freight refund. Cargo insurance is available at 1% of cargo value and can cover full cargo value compensation according to the insurance arrangement.

Should I buy cargo insurance?

Insurance is recommended for high-value cargo, fragile cargo or important shipments. If you need full cargo value protection, please ask us before shipping.

What should I do if tracking is delayed?

Tracking may be delayed during warehouse processing, customs clearance, flight/sea departure, port processing or local delivery transfer. Send us your tracking number and we will help check the status.
