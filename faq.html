Shipping time & service options

How long does DDP shipping from China take?

Express shipping is usually faster, air freight DDP is usually a balanced option, and sea freight DDP is usually better for heavier or non-urgent cargo. The final timeline depends on destination, cargo type, customs inspection, delivery area and local carrier transfer.

Which service should I choose: air, sea or express?

Choose express for urgent samples and small parcels, air freight DDP for a balance of speed and cost, and sea freight DDP for heavier cargo, bulk inventory or non-urgent shipments.

Do you provide door-to-door delivery?

Yes. We can arrange door-to-door shipping from China to a home, office, warehouse or Amazon FBA address. When DDP service is available, customs clearance, tax handling and final delivery are included in the shipping solution.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes. We support Amazon FBA shipments, including carton receiving, checking, labeling support and delivery to Amazon warehouses when the delivery option and cargo are suitable.

DDP, tax & customs clearance

What does DDP shipping mean?

DDP means Delivered Duty Paid. For available DDP services, the shipping solution includes customs clearance and import tax handling, helping the cargo reach the delivery address more easily.

Is tax and customs clearance included?

For supported DDP services, customs clearance and import tax handling are included in the shipping solution. Final availability and pricing depend on cargo details, destination, route and compliance review.

Do I need to handle customs myself?

For DDP service, we help arrange customs clearance and tax handling as part of the shipping solution. For special cargo, extra product information, documents or photos may be required before shipping.

Can the price change after customs or cargo review?

If the cargo details are different from the information provided before shipping, the delivery option or cost may need to be adjusted. Accurate product details, carton size and weight help avoid price changes.

Accepted goods & prohibited goods

What goods can you usually ship?

We can usually help with general goods, e-commerce products, clothing, accessories, toys, household items, samples, personal parcels and many business shipments.

Can you ship cosmetics, batteries or branded goods?

Many sensitive goods can be handled after route and compliance checking. Please send product photos, packaging details, product description, quantity and destination before shipping.

What goods cannot be shipped?

We cannot accept illegal goods, controlled substances, stolen items, weapons, ammunition, military supplies, explosives, dangerous goods or items that fail customs, safety or route compliance review.

Can you ship liquid, powder or magnetic products?

Some liquid, powder or magnetic products may be possible after review. Do not ship these items without checking first, because restrictions can be different by product, destination and carrier.

Quote, pickup & warehouse support

How can I get an accurate shipping quote?

Please provide destination country, city or ZIP code, product name, product photos, carton quantity, carton size, gross weight, supplier city and preferred shipping method. A packing list is helpful if available.

Can you pick up goods from my supplier in China?

Yes. We can arrange pickup support across mainland China. Your supplier can also send the goods to our Yiwu warehouse for receiving, checking, packing or consolidation.

Can you consolidate goods from multiple suppliers?

Yes. We can receive goods from multiple suppliers in China, check and consolidate them before arranging international shipping.

Can you help with inspection, packing and labeling?

Yes. We can help with basic inspection, repacking, carton checking, labeling and preparation support. Please send your requirements before the cargo arrives at the warehouse.

Tracking, insurance & support

How can I track my shipment?

You can use the tracking page on our website or contact us with your tracking number. Tracking information may take time to update after pickup, customs clearance or transfer to the local carrier.

What should I do if tracking is delayed?

Tracking may be delayed during warehouse processing, customs clearance, flight or sea departure, port processing or local delivery transfer. Send us your tracking number and we will help check the status.

What happens if cargo is lost or damaged?

For standard compensation, air freight compensation is 40 RMB/kg plus freight refund, and sea freight compensation is 20 RMB/kg plus freight refund. Cargo insurance is available at 1% of cargo value and can cover full cargo value compensation according to the insurance arrangement.

Should I buy cargo insurance?

Insurance is recommended for high-value cargo, fragile cargo or important shipments. If you need full cargo value protection, please ask us before shipping.
