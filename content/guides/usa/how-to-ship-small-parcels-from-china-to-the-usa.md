<h2>Which shipping route is suitable for a small parcel from China to the USA?</h2>

<p>For small parcels, samples and e-commerce restocking shipments, the most suitable route usually depends on the product type, chargeable weight, carton dimensions, required delivery speed and the final USA ZIP code.</p>

<p>There is no single route that is best for every parcel. Before confirming the shipment, it is important to compare the available options and check whether the goods contain batteries, liquids, powders, magnets, branded items or other special materials.</p>

<h3>Express DDP for urgent small parcels</h3>

<p>Express DDP is often suitable for urgent samples, personal parcels and time-sensitive shipments. For eligible cargo, the estimated delivery time is usually about 1–3 or 5–7 working days, depending on the route.</p>

<p>This option normally costs more than air DDP, but it can be useful when speed is the main priority.</p>

<h3>Air DDP for a balance of speed and cost</h3>

<p>Air DDP is commonly used for cartons, small commercial parcels and e-commerce restocking shipments. For eligible cargo, the estimated delivery time is usually about 7–12 or 12–15 days, depending on the route.</p>

<p>For many small and medium-size shipments, air DDP provides a practical balance between delivery time and shipping cost.</p>

<h3>Sea DDP for heavier, non-urgent cargo</h3>

<p>Sea DDP is generally more suitable for heavier cartons, wholesale goods and planned inventory shipments. The estimated delivery time is usually about 25–60 days, depending on the vessel schedule, customs processing and the final delivery state.</p>

<p>For a very small parcel, sea freight may not always be the best option. However, it can become more cost-effective when the shipment is heavier and the delivery is not urgent.</p>

<h2>Can parcels from multiple Chinese suppliers be consolidated?</h2>

<p>Yes. Goods from multiple suppliers can be sent to our China warehouse before international shipping. We can help receive the packages, check the carton condition, take photos, consolidate parcels and repack the goods when needed.</p>

<p>Consolidation is useful when you purchase products from several suppliers on platforms such as 1688, Taobao or other Chinese sourcing channels. Instead of shipping each parcel separately, the goods can be reviewed together and arranged under a suitable shipping route.</p>

<ul>
