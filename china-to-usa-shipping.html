Shipping from China to the USA

Door-to-door shipping support from Chinese suppliers to a US home, business address, warehouse or Amazon FBA warehouse. We help check suitable air, sea, express and DDP options based on cargo details.

What we can help with

If you buy goods from Chinese suppliers, we can help receive the cargo in China, check package condition, consolidate suppliers, repack cartons when needed and arrange a suitable shipping option to the USA.

  • Supplier pickup or warehouse receiving in China
  • Package checking, consolidation and repacking
  • Air freight, sea freight, express and DDP options when suitable
  • Delivery to home, business address, warehouse or Amazon FBA
  • Tracking and delivery follow-up support

Details needed for a quote

  • Product name and photos
  • Carton quantity, size and gross weight
  • Supplier city or pickup address in China
  • US delivery city, state, ZIP code and address type
  • Whether the goods include battery, liquid, powder, magnet or brand items

A final quote depends on product type, weight, volume, destination ZIP code, delivery method and customs requirements.

Common China to USA shipping options

Express

Suitable for samples, urgent parcels and smaller shipments when speed is more important than the lowest cost.

Air DDP

Suitable for many e-commerce restock shipments and medium cartons when a balance of speed and cost is needed.

Sea DDP

Suitable for heavier cartons and bulk cargo when the shipment is planned and cost control matters more than speed.

Frequently asked questions

Can you ship to a US home address?

Yes, when the delivery option and cargo type are suitable, we can arrange delivery to a home address, business address, warehouse or Amazon FBA address.

Can you pick up goods from my supplier in China?

Yes. You can send us the supplier contact or pickup address. We can coordinate receiving or pickup before shipping.

Can you handle small packages?

Small packages can be checked. The best option depends on weight, product type and urgency. Very small shipments often have a higher unit cost.

Is DDP always available?

Not always. DDP availability depends on cargo type, destination ZIP code and current route conditions. We will check before quoting.
