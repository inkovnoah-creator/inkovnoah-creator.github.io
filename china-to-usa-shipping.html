Choose the right China to USA route.

USA shipments can use different routes depending on cargo type, chargeable weight, carton size, timing and final address. The time ranges below are practical references, not fixed promises.

Fastest option

Express DDP to USA

For urgent samples, small parcels and time-sensitive shipments.

  • Estimated time: about 1–3 or 5–7 working days, depending on route
  • DDP / tax-included option can be checked when the route and cargo are suitable
  • Higher cost, better for small urgent shipments
Balanced route

Air DDP to USA

For cartons, e-commerce restocking and medium-size shipments.

  • Estimated time: about 7–12 or 12–15 days, depending on route
  • Good balance of speed and cost for many USA shipments
  • Final delivery can be arranged to a home, office, warehouse or FBA address when suitable
Planned cargo

Sea DDP to USA

For heavier planned cargo, wholesale cartons and Amazon FBA shipments.

  • Estimated time: about 25–60 days, depending on vessel schedule and final state
  • Better unit cost for heavier, non-urgent shipments
  • Suitable for planned inventory and warehouse delivery
