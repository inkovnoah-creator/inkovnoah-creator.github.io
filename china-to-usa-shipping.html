How the China to USA shipping process works.

We do not only provide a freight price. We help organize the steps before international shipping so the cargo can move more smoothly.

1. Send detailsProduct photos, weight, carton size, supplier city and USA destination.
2. Receive cargoSupplier can send goods to our China warehouse or we can check pickup options.
3. Check and packWe can check carton condition, consolidate and repack when needed.
4. Ship and deliverChoose a suitable route and arrange final delivery to the USA address.

Questions about shipping from China to the USA.

Can you help with Amazon FBA?

Yes. We can help with warehouse receiving, carton checking and delivery planning for FBA shipments when the required labels and shipment information are provided.

We can give a reference quote based on product type, weight, carton size and destination. The final quote should be based on actual warehouse data.
