Choose the right China to USA route for your cargo.

Different products, weights and addresses need different routes. The time ranges below are practical references. Final availability depends on cargo type, carton data, customs situation, flight or vessel schedule, and the final USA ZIP code.

Fastest option

Express DDP shipping

For urgent samples, small parcels and time-sensitive goods. When the cargo is suitable, express can be the fastest China to USA option.

  • Fastest express: about 1–3 working days
  • Regular express: about 5–7 working days
  • DDP / tax-included option when cargo and route allow
Balanced speed

Air DDP to USA

For cartons and restocking shipments where express is too expensive but sea freight is too slow.

  • Faster air DDP: about 7–12 days
  • Standard air DDP: about 12–15 days
  • Final USA delivery by UPS, FedEx or suitable local carrier
Bulk cargo

Sea DDP to USA

For heavier cartons, planned inventory, warehouse delivery and Amazon FBA shipments where cost control is more important.

  • Fast ocean, standard ocean and economy ocean options
  • USA warehouse pickup before local delivery
  • Suitable for many warehouse and FBA deliveries

How air DDP and sea DDP delivery works.

For many USA shipments, the process is not only international transportation. It also includes China warehouse handling, export preparation, customs support and final delivery coordination.

1. China pickup or receivingSupplier can send goods to our China warehouse, or we check pickup options based on supplier address.
2. Check and prepareWe can help check carton condition, consolidate goods, repack and prepare labels if needed.
3. International transportExpress, air DDP or sea DDP will be selected based on cargo type, weight and timing.
4. USA final deliveryAfter arrival and processing in the USA, final delivery can be arranged to home, office, warehouse or Amazon FBA when suitable.

What DDP means for USA shipments.

When a USA route can be quoted as DDP, the quote is intended to include international shipping, customs/tax handling and delivery to the confirmed address. Availability must be checked by cargo type and ZIP code before shipment.
