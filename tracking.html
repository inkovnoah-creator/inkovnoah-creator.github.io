Tracking FAQ

Common questions when checking China-to-USA and international shipment updates.

Why is my tracking number not showing updates?

Tracking updates may take time to appear after shipment pickup, warehouse processing, customs clearance or transfer to the final delivery carrier. Please check again later or contact us with your tracking number.

How many tracking numbers can I check at once?

You can enter up to 10 tracking numbers at once. Use one number per line, or separate numbers by commas, spaces or semicolons.

What should I do if the tracking result looks delayed?

Some routes may have slower updates during customs clearance, flight or sea departure, port processing or local delivery transfer. Send us your tracking number and we can help check the status.

Can I track air, sea and express shipments here?

Yes. You can use this page to check tracking numbers for air freight, sea freight and express shipments handled by HC Freight.

Need help with your tracking number?

Send your tracking number to us by WhatsApp or email. We will help check the shipment status.
