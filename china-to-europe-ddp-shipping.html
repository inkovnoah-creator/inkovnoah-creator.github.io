Questions about China to Europe shipping.

Quick answers for EU destination enquiries and the separate UK route.

Do you provide DDP shipping from China to Germany, France, Italy and Spain?

Yes. We can review tax-included DDP options for suitable cargo. Send the product name, photos, carton quantity, carton dimensions, gross weight and the final delivery postcode.

Can I use the Europe page for the Netherlands, Poland, Romania and other listed destinations?

Yes. The Europe page covers the listed EU destinations. Route availability and the final quote still depend on the exact postcode, address type, cargo details and current schedule.

Can you receive goods from multiple suppliers in China?

Yes. Suppliers can send goods to our China warehouse. We can help with receiving, package checking, consolidation, repacking and shipment preparation before dispatch.
