Accepted cargo

We accept a wide range of cargo types. Please send photos and product details before shipping, especially for sensitive items.

  • General cargo: clothes, toys, household items, electronics, accessories and daily necessities
  • Most sensitive cargo types: cosmetics, skincare, makeup, lithium-ion batteries and personal care products
  • Branded and name-brand goods with specialized handling after review
  • Bulk and e-commerce cargo, including Amazon FBA, LCL/FCL and commercial shipments
  • Personal parcels, gifts and personal effects from China to the USA

Prohibited cargo

For safety and legal compliance, some items cannot be accepted. If your cargo is not listed, send details for checking first.

  • Hazardous or dangerous goods such as flammable, explosive, corrosive, toxic or radioactive materials
  • Military supplies, weapons, ammunition and drones
  • Illegal items, stolen merchandise or controlled substances
  • Items that fail customs, safety or route compliance review

Service FAQ

Common questions before choosing air freight, sea freight or express shipping.

Which service should I choose?

Choose express for the fastest small shipments, air freight DDP for a balance of speed and cost, and sea freight DDP for larger non-urgent cargo.

Does DDP include customs clearance and tax handling?

Yes. DDP service includes customs clearance and import tax handling as part of the shipping solution. Final pricing depends on cargo type, route and destination.

Can you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes. We support Amazon FBA shipments, including labeling and warehouse appointment support when needed.

Can you handle batteries, cosmetics or branded goods?

Many sensitive cargo types can be handled after review. Please send product photos, packaging details and quantity before shipping.

Can you pick up from my supplier in China?

Yes. Door pickup is available across mainland China. Your supplier can also send goods to our Yiwu warehouse for receiving, checking and consolidation.
