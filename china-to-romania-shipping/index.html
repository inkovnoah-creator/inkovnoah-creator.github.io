Planned cargo

Rail DDP to Romania

For non-urgent general cargo that needs a door-to-door solution with a planned timeline.

Lower cost

For heavier, bulkier and non-urgent shipments where cost is more important than speed.

  • Suitable for planned inventory and bulkier cargo
  • Best when the customer can wait longer for a lower-cost route
