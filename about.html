Who we are

We make international shipping from China easier to manage.

Many customers do not want to manage suppliers, warehouses, customs brokers, duty payment and final delivery separately. HC Freight helps combine these steps into a clearer door-to-door shipping solution.

Our team supports cargo receiving, route checking, packing, consolidation, customs clearance and delivery coordination for general goods, e-commerce inventory, FBA shipments, personal parcels and selected sensitive cargo after review.

  • Clear route recommendation based on cargo type and destination
  • Air, sea and express shipping options
  • DDP service with customs and tax handling for supported routes
  • China pickup, warehouse receiving and shipment preparation support
